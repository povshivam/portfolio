Testing & Quarantine

Quarantine is a period when you get exposed to a person with covid 19 symptoms or positive test of COVID 19. You need to keep yourself quarantine in separate room in for 14 days. Post-Infection

You might remain positive for COVID 19 PCR for the next 90 days. So please do not retest yourself for the next 90 days or until you have new respiratory symptoms or shortness of breath. You might have long haul covid which could require you to use supplement oxygen for prolonged periods of time.
